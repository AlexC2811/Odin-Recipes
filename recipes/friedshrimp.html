Fried Shrimp

Fried shrimp are shrimp that have been coated in batter and then deep or pan fried. they can be served as either a main course or appetizer based on the way that they are prepared

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Black pepper
  • Granulated garlic
  • Paprika
  • Oregano
  • Shrimp
  • Flour
  • Spices of choice
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Soak the shrimp by mixing milk, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, black pepper, half the granulated garlic, and half of the oregano. Add shrimp in and soak for 10 minutes.
  2. Mix the spices and flour on a plate and coat the shrimp well. Make sure to coat the shrimp well. Coat several times.
  3. Heat the oil to 375 and fry each batch of shrimp for about 1-2 minutes.
  4. Allow for shrimp to cool and serve.
