frittata

Frittata is an egg based dish that originated in italy. It is a versatile dish as it can be prepared with various different meats or vegetables

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon seasalt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 scallions
  • 2 cups of chopped broccoli
  • 1/8 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Whisk the eggs, almond milk, and salt until well combined.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a cast iron skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the scallions, brocolli, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Stir occasionally until the broccoli is tender but still bright green (5-8 minutes).
  6. Stir in the smoked paprika, then add egg mixture and gently shake pan
  7. Sprinkle with the feta and bake 15-20 minutes.
  8. Season to taste and serve.
